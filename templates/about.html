About Wolken Software

Empowering enterprises with intelligent, scalable, and intuitive customer service solutions.

Who We Are

Wolken Software is a leading provider of cloud-based Customer Service and IT Service Management solutions. Founded with a vision to redefine enterprise support systems, Wolken empowers organizations to deliver faster, smarter, and more personalized customer experiences — all through a secure, modern, and flexible platform.

Our Vision

To transform enterprise service ecosystems through innovation, automation, and human-centric design — helping businesses engage meaningfully and respond instantly.

Our Mission

To provide cloud-native service management solutions that simplify workflows, enhance collaboration, and deliver measurable business outcomes.

Our Core Values

  • Innovation: Constantly pushing boundaries with next-generation technology.
  • Integrity: Building trust with transparency and accountability.
  • Customer Success: Placing client outcomes at the center of everything we do.
  • Collaboration: Creating impact through teamwork and shared vision.

Our Products

Wolken offers two enterprise-grade products — Wolken Customer ServiceDesk and Wolken IT ServiceDesk — designed to streamline operations, reduce resolution time, and improve service quality. Both solutions are scalable and align seamlessly with modern digital ecosystems.

Leadership

Led by seasoned professionals with decades of experience in technology and service delivery, Wolken’s leadership team is committed to building a culture of excellence and customer-first innovation.

Global Presence

Headquartered in Bangalore, India, Wolken has a growing international footprint with clients across North America, Europe, and Asia-Pacific. We serve enterprises across multiple industries — from IT and Telecom to Banking, Healthcare, and Retail.
